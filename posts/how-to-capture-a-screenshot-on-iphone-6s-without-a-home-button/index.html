Q1: How do I take a screenshot on my iPhone 6s without a Home button? A: To take a screenshot on your iPhone 6s without a Home button, press and hold the Side button (formerly known as the Sleep/Wake button) and the Volume Up button at the same time. You should hear a sound (if your sound is enabled) and see a thumbnail of the screenshot appear in the lower-left corner of the screen.

Q2: How do I view my screenshot? A: To view your screenshot, tap the thumbnail in the bottom-left corner of the screen. This will open the Photos app and show you the screenshot. You can then share or edit the screenshot as you like.

Q3: How do I share my screenshot? A: You can share your screenshot by tapping the Share button in the Photos app. From there, you can share your screenshot with AirDrop, Messages, Mail, Slack, and other services.

Q4: How do I delete a screenshot? A: To delete a screenshot, open the Photos app and tap the screenshot. Then tap the Trash icon in the bottom-right corner and confirm that you want to delete the screenshot.

Q5: What other methods can I use to take a screenshot? A: If you don’t have an iPhone 6s, you can still take a screenshot with an older iPhone model. On older iPhones, press and hold the Home button and the Sleep/Wake button simultaneously. On iPhone X and newer models, press and hold the Side button and the Volume Up button.
