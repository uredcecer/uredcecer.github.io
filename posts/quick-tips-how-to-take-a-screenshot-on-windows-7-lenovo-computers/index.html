To take a screenshot on your Windows 7 Lenovo computer, here are the quick tips to follow:

First, press the Print Screen (PrtSc) button on your keyboard. This will capture the screen of your entire active window. Next, open an image-editing software like MS Paint or Adobe Photoshop, and paste (Ctrl+V) the image of the screenshot. You can then edit the screenshot and save it in any image format like JPEG or PNG.

Another way to take a screenshot is to press the Windows + Print Screen (PrtSc) keys simultaneously. This will direct the screenshot file to save as PNG in the Pictures > Screenshots folder. You can open the image directly from the folder into an image-editing software, such as MS Paint or Photoshop, and edit it as desired.

In addition to the Print Screen key, you can also use the Snipping Tool available on the Windows 7 computers. The tool allows you to create a screenshot of the desired part of the screen. To access the tool, type “Snipping Tool” into the search box in the Start menu and press Enter. After launching the tool, click and drag your cursor to select the desired part of the screen and the screenshot will be saved on your computer.

Finally, if you have a Lenovo laptop, you can also press the FN + PrtSc to take a screenshot. This method works the same as the Windows and PrtSc key combination; the screenshot will be saved directly into the Pictures > Screenshots folder as a PNG file.
