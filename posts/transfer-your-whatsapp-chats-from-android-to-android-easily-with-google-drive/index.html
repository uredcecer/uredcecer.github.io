A. If you switch from an Android device to an iOS device, your WhatsApp chats will not be transferred as it is not possible to transfer chats between operating systems. You will need to manually back up your chats before switching by going to Settings > Chats > Chat Backup and tapping on Back Up.

Q.4 Can I back up my WhatsApp chats to my computer using Google Drive?

A. No, unfortunately it is not possible to back up your WhatsApp chats to your computer using Google Drive. You can only back up your WhatsApp chats to your Google Drive account.
