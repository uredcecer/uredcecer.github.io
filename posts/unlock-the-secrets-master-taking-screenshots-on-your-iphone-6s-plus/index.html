Q4: Can I transfer screenshots from my iPhone 6s Plus to my computer? A4: Yes, you can! You can connect your iPhone to your computer using the USB cable and then drag and drop the screenshots directly from the Photos app into your computer’s file system.

Q5: Is there any way to take a screenshot without using the physical buttons? A5: Yes, there is! You can use the AssistiveTouch feature on your iPhone 6s Plus to take a screenshot without using the physical buttons. To use AssistiveTouch, head to the Settings app, tap on “General”, and then tap “Accessibility”. Enable AssistiveTouch and add the “Screenshot” shortcut to the AssistiveTouch menu. Then, whenever you want to take a screenshot, just tap on the AssistiveTouch button and select “Screenshot”.
