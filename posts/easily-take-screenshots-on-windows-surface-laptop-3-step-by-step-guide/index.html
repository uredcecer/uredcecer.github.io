Q1: What is the easiest way to take screenshots on Windows Surface Laptop 3?

A1: To take screenshots on Windows Surface Laptop 3, the easiest way is to press the “Windows + Volume Down” buttons simultaneously. This will take a screenshot of the whole screen and it will be saved to the Pictures > Screenshots folder.

Q2: What is the alternative method to take screenshots on Windows Surface Laptop 3?

A2: You can also use the Snipping Tool to take screenshots on Windows Surface Laptop 3. To open the Snipping Tool, locate it in the Windows Search bar, or type ‘Snipping Tool’ into the search bar. The Snipping Tool will let you choose which part of your screen you wish to snip and save.

Q3: How can I annotate the screenshots on Windows Surface Laptop 3?

A3: The Snipping Tool also allows you to annotate your screenshots. Once you capture a snip, you can use the Snipping Tool’s drawing tools to add a rectangle, ellipse, highlight, or free-form shape to your screenshot. You can also add text to the screenshot.

Q4: How can I take a screenshot of a single window on Windows Surface Laptop 3?

A4: To take a screenshot of a single window, press the ‘Alt + Print Screen’ buttons simultaneously. This will only capture the active window instead of the entire screen. To locate the screenshot, go to the Pictures > Screenshots folder.

Q5: How can I share the screenshots on Windows Surface Laptop 3?

A5: To share the screenshots on Windows Surface Laptop 3, you can use the Share charm. Simply press the ‘Windows + H’ buttons simultaneously and select the destination for the screenshot such as an email or social media, and it will be shared to the selected destination.
