1. Introduction to Screen Mirroring#

Screen mirroring is the technique of projecting the mobile screen onto a Computer, TV, or another gadget. It’s a great feature for presentations, and it’s also useful for staying connected with family and friends. This feature is available on MI Android TVs, so here’s a step-by-step guide on how to enable it. First, you need to make sure that the MI TV and your mobile phone are connected to the same Wi-Fi network. Then, open the Setting app on your MI TV and look for the Screen Mirroring section. Here, enable the option and connect to your device. Your mobile screen should appear on the TV. To stop the screen mirroring, just disable the option from the Settings menu. Screen mirroring is a great way to watch movies or TV from your phone, or even play games with a bigger screen. Enjoy the convenience of screen mirroring with your MI TV.

2. Setting Up Screen Mirroring#

Screen mirroring is a technology supported by many modern Android smartphones that allows you to easily mirror the display from your device to a Mi Android TV. You can easily share pictures, webpages, YouTube videos, and more from your phone or tablet to your TV. This guide will show you how to set it up and use it.

First, make sure your TV is connected to the same Wi-Fi network as your device. On your device, open the Settings app and look for the Display tab. Find the “Screen Mirroring” option and make sure it is enabled. You will then be asked to select the TV you want to connect to.

Once your device has connected to the Mi Android TV, you will be able to easily stream content from your device directly to the TV. To begin streaming, open the app you wish to share and look for the Cast icon. Tap the Cast icon, and the display on your device will be sent to the TV.

When you are done streaming, simply go back to the Display tab in the Settings app and toggle off the Screen Mirroring option. Alternatively, you can also disconnect from the TV by going to the TV’s settings and choosing “Disconnect” from the device list.

Screen mirroring is a great way to share content between your device and your Mi Android TV. With just a few simple steps, you can quickly and easily share your favorite photos, webpages, and videos with your friends and family.
