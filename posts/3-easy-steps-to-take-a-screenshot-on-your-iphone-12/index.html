Q.1 How do I take a screenshot on my iPhone 12? A. Taking a screenshot on your iPhone 12 is easy, all you have to do is press the Volume Up and the Side buttons simultaneously and release them right away.

Q.2 What do I do with the screenshot after taking it? A. After taking a screenshot, you can instantly view it in the bottom left corner of your iPhone’s screen or in the Screenshots album in the Photos app. You can also share, edit, or delete the screenshot from the app.

Q.3 What if I press the Power button instead? A. If you press the Power button instead of the Volume Up and Side buttons, your iPhone 12 will lock and the screenshot will not be taken.

Q.4 What other ways are there to take a screenshot? A. In addition to taking a screenshot with the Volume Up and Side buttons, you can also take a screenshot with AssistiveTouch. To activate AssistiveTouch, go to Settings > Accessibility > Touch > AssistiveTouch and select “On”. Then, you can take a screenshot by simply tapping the AssistiveTouch icon and then selecting “Screenshot”.

Q.5 Is the process the same for other iPhone models? A. No, the process of taking a screenshot may vary slightly for different iPhone models. For example, for the iPhone 8 and earlier, the process involves pressing the Power button and Home button instead of the Volume Up and Side buttons.
