1.Cara menggunakan tombol ‘Ctl+Window Switcher’#

Taking screenshots on your HP Stream Chromebook is incredibly easy. All you need to do is press and hold the ‘Ctrl + Window Switcher’ keys. Doing this will create a screenshot of the current screen–which you can then save in the Downloads folder. It’s also possible to take a screenshot of a specific part of the screen, rather than the whole thing. To do this, press and hold the ‘Ctrl’ and ‘Shift’ keys and then press the ‘Window Switcher’ key. Your cursor will then turn into a crosshair - allowing you to drag and select the area you want to capture. Once you release the mouse button, the screenshot will be saved in the Downloads folder. It’s worth noting that the file is in PNG format, not JPEG. Finally, you can also take a screenshot of an entire page. To do this, press and hold the ‘Ctrl’ and ‘Shift’ keys, and press the ‘Fn’ key. Your screenshot will then be saved in the Downloads folder.
