1. How to Access the TV Setup Screen#

Accessing the TV setup screen on a TCL Android TV is relatively simple. To start, press the power button located on the remote control and the Android TV box will boot up. Once the home screen appears, select the Settings icon from the top menu. From the settings menu, select the System option and then select TV Inputs from the sub-menu. This will bring up the TV setup screen, where you can begin scanning and setting up TV channels.

2. Select Channel Scan Mode.#

Accessing the TV setup screen on your TCL Android TV is easy. All you have to do is press the ‘Home’ button on your remote control and select ‘Settings’ from the home menu. This will take you to the TV setup screen where you can begin scanning for channels. From here, select the option for ‘Scan for Channels,’ and the TV will begin searching for available channels. Once the scan is complete, you will have access to your new digital channels.

3. Setting Options Based on Mode.#

Accessing the TV Setup screen on a TCL Android TV is easy. All you need to do is press the Home button on your remote control. Doing so will open the device’s user interface, and from there, you can easily select Settings, and then select TV Setup. This screen will provide you with the options to manually scan for channels, as well as the ability to choose the type of connection you are using. Follow this guide to learn how to successfully scan channels on your TCL Android TV.

4. Start Channel Scan.#

Accessing the TV setup screen on TCL Android TV is relatively simple. By pressing the ‘Settings’ icon on the main page, users will be presented with the setup menu. From there, selecting the ‘External Device Setup’ tab will show the options for channel tuning. Once within the ‘External Device Setup’ menu, users must select ‘TV Setup’ and the channel tuning option will appear. By selecting ‘Manual Setup’, users can start the search for available channels. It is also possible to refine the types of channels with the ‘Channel Type’ option. The channel scan process will then begin automatically. During the scan, the TV will display the total number of channels found. After that, users can begin watching the available channels.

5. Finalize TV Setup.#

Accessing the TV Setup Screen on your TCL Android TV is easy. To begin, press the Home button on your remote and scroll to the Settings menu in the top right corner. Under the Settings menu, you’ll find a TV Setup option. Selecting this option will take you to the TV Setup Screen. Here you’ll find options for setting up your TV size, connected devices, time zone, and other preferences. You’ll also find the TV Setup menu which is where you can find the options for scanning your channels.

2. How to Set Up TV Signal#

To get started with a TCL Android TV, you first need to set up the TV signal. To do this, navigate to the Settings menu and select Channels. Then, select Scan for Channels and put in the required information for your region. This should automatically scan for available channels. Once scanning is complete, select Save to save the list of available channels. You can then select the channels you want to watch. Additionally, you can organize your channels into lists for easier navigation.

3. How to Save and Rescan TV Channels#

Saving and rescannning TV channels on your TCL Android TV is a straightforward process and can come in handy in case you experience poor TV reception due to signal interference. To save and rescan your TV channels, you’ll need to access the TV’s settings menu and then follow the steps. First, access the ‘Settings’ menu on the TCL Android TV and then select ‘Channels’. This will allow you to view any already saved TV channels and to rescan for additional channels. Next, select the ‘Auto-Scan’ option, which instructs the TV to search for available channels. You can select between antenna and cable TV channels depending on your source of reception. Finally, after the scan is done, you can select any new channels by checking the ‘Channel’ box and then select ‘Apply’ to save the changes. You can also use the ‘Advanced Settings’ option to fine-tune the settings according to your needs.

1. Saving TV Channels#

Saving and rescanning of your TV channels on a TCL Android TV can be done easily and quickly. To begin, press the Home button on your TCL Android TV remote and choose Settings. From there, you can access the Live TV menu. Select the Scan button and choose the network you want to rescan for new channels. The system will then search for and store available channels. When the scan is complete, the View Channels screen will show available channels and you can start watching them.

It’s also possible to delete previously saved channels. To do that, select the View Channels screen, choose the channel you’d like to remove, and press the Delete button on the remote. The channel will be deleted from your list and won’t be available anymore.

Additionally, most TCL Android TV series have a Live TV app that collects and organizes all the big online streaming services in one place. With the Live TV app, you can select, delete, and reorder your favorite channels with just a few taps.

Finally, you can also save and rescan a specific channel with a single tap. To do so, press the Home button on your remote and choose the List View. Then, select the channel you’d like to rescan and press the OK button. The system will then search for available signals and store them. When it’s done, you can watch your favorite channel again.

2. Rescanning TV Channels#

To scan channels on your TCL Android TV, first select the ‘Settings’ option in your home screen. Then scroll down to ‘TV Sources’ and click on ‘TV’ to find the ‘Auto Tuning’ option. In the Auto Tuning window, select ‘Add Channels’ and then click on ‘Start’ to start the scanning process. Once the scanning is complete, you will have a list of all available channels. To save the scanned channels, select ‘Save Channels’ in the Auto Tuning window, and all the channels will be saved to your TCL Android TV. To rescan for new channels, repeat the same process.

Q.1. What is the purpose of a TV channel scan?

A.1. The purpose of a TV channel scan is to search through all available broadcast TV channels available in your area, organizing them according to their frequency, and storing them in your TV memory. This will enable you to easily access the TV channels you receive from your antenna or cable provider whenever you want.

Q.2. How do I know if my TCL Android TV supports channel scanning? A.2. You can check if your TCL Android TV supports channel scanning by looking up the model number in the product manual. You can also check on the manufacturer’s website or ask their customer service to confirm if channel scanning is supported on your device.

Q.3. What hardware do I need to perform a channel scan on my TCL Android TV? A.3. To perform a channel scan on your TCL Android TV, you will need a digital antenna and an active internet connection. If you are using cable or satellite, you may also need an additional tuner box.

Q.4. Is it possible to perform a channel scan while connected to a Wi-Fi network? A.4. Yes, it is possible to perform a channel scan while connected to a Wi-Fi network. However, in order to receive a full range of available channels, it is recommended to connect the antenna directly to the TV using an Ethernet cable.

Q.5. What happens after I have successfully completed a channel scan on my TCL Android TV? A.5. After you have successfully completed a channel scan on your TCL Android TV, all the available channels in your area should be stored in your TV’s memory. You can then access these channels anytime by going to the “TV Channels” menu on your TV.
