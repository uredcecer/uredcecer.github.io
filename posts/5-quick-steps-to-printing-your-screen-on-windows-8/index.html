1. Getting to Know the Print Screen Button#

Press the Print Screen button on your keyboard. This will take an image of the entire screen. The image will be stored as a screenshot in the Pictures library. To access the screenshot, navigate to the Pictures library in your Start menu and select it.

Alternatively, press the Alt + Print Screen buttons simultaneously. This will take a snapshot of the active window on your screen, without bringing up the Print Screen dialog box. The image will be stored in the same manner as with pressing the Print Screen button.

The final Print Screen method you can use is the Snipping Tool. This will allow you to take a screenshot of any window, as well as take a free-form screenshot of any area. To find the Snipping Tool, search for it in the Start Menu.

Overall, taking a screenshot on a Windows 8 PC is easy and convenient. Utilizing all the Print Screen methods will help you capture any image that’s on your screen.
