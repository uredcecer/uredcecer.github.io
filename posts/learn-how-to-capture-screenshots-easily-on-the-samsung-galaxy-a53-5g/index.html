You can also capture screenshots using voice commands. To do this, go to the Quick Settings menu and tap on the Bixby Routines option. Then, tap on Bixby Voice Wake-Up and say Take a screenshot. The same shutter sound and white border will appear, confirming that the screenshot has been taken.

The Samsung Galaxy A53 5G also allows users to capture scrolling or long screenshots. To do this go to the Quick Settings menu, and select the Smart Capture option. Select Capture Long Screenshot and it will automatically scroll down the page and capture the entire scrolling page.

Finally, you can also take screenshots with the S Pen stylus. Hold down the stylus’ button until the Air Command menu appears. Select “Screen Write” and the screenshot will be saved to the Gallery.
