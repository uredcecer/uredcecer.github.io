3. Capturing a Screenshot on iPhone 8 Plus#

Taking screenshots on an iPhone 8 Plus without the side button is easy, and can be done by using the AssistiveTouch feature. AssistiveTouch creates an on-screen home button that can be used to access various functions in iOS, including taking screenshots. To enable this feature, open the Settings app, tap General, then tap Accessibility. Under the Interaction section, tap AssistiveTouch to turn it on.

After enabling AssistiveTouch, a small icon will appear on the screen that can be used to access several functions. To take a screenshot, tap the AssistiveTouch icon, then select the Device option. From the Device menu, select More, then Screenshot. This will take a screenshot and save it in the Photos app.

To customize the AssistiveTouch icon, go back into the Accessibility settings, select AssistiveTouch, then tap Customize Top Level Menu. Here, you can add or remove various controls. By default, the Screenshot icon is present, but you can also add shortcuts for other functions such as volume, rotation lock, and brightness.

AssistiveTouch is a convenient way to take screenshots on an iPhone 8 Plus without the need for a side button. All the screenshots taken this way are saved in the Photos app, and can be easily shared with friends and family.
