It’s great for when you need to save or share an image quickly, or for saving records of important documents and information. Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to do it:

First, you’ll want to make sure that whatever you want to take a screenshot of is visible on your screen. Then, locate the Print Screen key (it’s usually abbreviated to PrtSc) on your keyboard. Once you’ve found it, press it. This will take a snapshot of everything that is on your screen.

Next, open up Microsoft Paint. You can find this on the Start Menu by typing in the program’s name. Once Paint is open, press Ctrl + V to paste your screenshot. Now, you’ll be able to edit or resize it. When you’re finished, choose the “File” tab and select “Save as” to give your screenshot a name and choose where you want to save it.

That’s it! You’ve just captured a screenshot on your Lenovo laptop running Windows 7. Now you can easily save or share images, records, or other information with others.
