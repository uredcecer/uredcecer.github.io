1. Enable Bluetooth on both iPhone and Android devices#

Enabling Bluetooth on both iPhone and Android devices is an important step in transferring photos from one device to another. This guide will show you how to quickly and easily enable Bluetooth on your devices so that you can begin transferring photos.

On your iPhone, open the Settings app and tap on Bluetooth. Make sure the switch is toggled to green. This will enable Bluetooth on your device.

On Android devices, first open the Notifications shade and then tap the Bluetooth icon to enable it. This will ensure that the Bluetooth on the Android device is turned on.

After both devices have Bluetooth enabled, you will be able to pair them together and begin transferring photos. To do this, tap the Pair button in the Bluetooth menu on both devices and when prompted, enter the same PIN. This will ensure that both devices recognize each other and are connected.

Once paired, select the photos you want to transfer and use the share menu in the Photos app to send them to the other device. The photos will begin transferring over Bluetooth and will arrive on the other device in just a few moments.
