Next, you should locate the “Voicemail Provider” option within the same page. This should be set to the default settings for your carrier. Simply select the option that reads “Voicemail Off” to disable any voicemail notifications.

The final step is to disable the “Auto Play” option. This will prevent your voicemail from being automatically played when you open the app. To disable the Auto Play feature, go to the Autoplay settings page and turn off the toggle labeled “Auto Play Messages.” This will ensure that you will no longer receive any unnecessary notifications.

By following these easy steps, you should now be able to easily turn off Visual Voicemail on your Android device. No longer will you be bombarded with unnecessary notifications and you can quickly check your voicemail without any hassle.
