Second, press and release the On/Off button located on the right-side of the device. This will capture the screenshot and place the image in the Photos app.

Third, press and drag the thumbnail located on the left corner of the display. Dragging the thumbnail will open the annotation tools, allowing you to draw or highlight on the image.

Finally, tap the Done button when you’ve finished annotating your screenshot to save the image to the Photos app. Now your screenshot is saved and ready to share with your friends.
