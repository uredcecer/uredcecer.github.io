Q1. What is the step-by-step guide to sharing the laptop screen with your TCL Android TV?

A1. Step-by-Step Guide to Sharing Laptop Screen with Your TCL Android TV:

  1. Turn on both devices, the laptop and the TCL Android TV.
  2. Connect the laptop to the TV using an HDMI cable.
  3. On your laptop, launch the “Settings” app.
  4. Click on “Displays” and select the “multi monitor mode”.
  5. Once the display mode is set, launch the “Miracast” app on your laptop.
  6. On your TCL Android TV, open the “Settings” menu, then navigate to “System” and select “Network & Internet”.
  7. Select the “Wi-Fi” option, and then select the “Miracast” option.
  8. A list of devices will appear on the screen. Select your laptop’s name from the list and you will be connected to the laptop.

Q2. What kind of cable required to connect the laptop and TCL Android TV?

A2. The most common cable used to connect the laptop and TCL Android TV is the HDMI cable. This cable will allow you to transfer audio and video signals from the laptop to the TV.

Q3. Does the TCL Android TV need to be connected to a Wi-Fi network to share laptop screen?

A3. Yes, the TCL Android TV needs to be connected to a Wi-Fi network in order to share the laptop screen.

Q4. Is it possible to share the laptop screen with multiple TCL Android TVs?

A4. Yes, it is possible to share the laptop screen with multiple TCL Android TVs. You will need to use a separate HDMI cable for each TV you want to connect to your laptop.

Q5. What is the “Multi Monitor Mode” used for?

A5. The “Multi Monitor Mode” is used to change the settings on your laptop’s display. By selecting the “Multi Monitor Mode”, you can choose to either extend your display, mirror your display, or use your laptop as a single display.
