Step 2: Press the Home and Sleep/Wake button simultaneously.

To capture a screenshot, lightly press and hold both the Home and Sleep/Wake button for 1-2 seconds. The screen will flash and the sound of a camera shutter will be heard indicating the screenshot has been taken.
